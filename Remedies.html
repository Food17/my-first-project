Remedies

Check our wide range of easy home Remedies

Hair Loss

Apply a mixture of coconut oil and onion juice to the scalp twice a week.

Massaging the scalp with warm coconut oil helps improve blood circulation, while onion juice contains sulfur, which promotes hair growth.

Leave the mixture on for at least 30 minutes before washing it off with a mild shampoo.

Graying

Massage your scalp with amla (Indian gooseberry) oil regularly.

Amla oil is rich in antioxidants and vitamin C, which helps prevent premature graying of hair and promotes hair growth.

Apply a small amount of amla oil to the scalp and hair, leave it on overnight, and wash it off the next morning.
